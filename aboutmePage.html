🤗 ABOUT ME


Hello, I'm Buse. I graduated from Bahçeşehir University software engineering department in 2023. During my studies, I did two one-year internships. The first one was as Data and IT intern at Doğuş Group-D.Serve. During this period, I developed tools using Python for process automation and also took part in project management processes. My second internship was in Boyner's Data Science team. Here I took part in projects such as NLP sentiment analysis, dynamic pricing, marketing mix modeling. I am currently working in the credit risk analytics department at ING Turkey. I also have a blog where I aim to share what I have learned during all these processes and deliver it to more people. I have been using this place for three years to take notes for myself and to deliver my notes to people. I hope you find the content interesting and useful.
